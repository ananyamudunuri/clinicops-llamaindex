Clinic Appointment and Billing Policy

Patients must arrive at least 15 minutes before their scheduled appointment.

If a patient cancels less than 24 hours before the appointment, the clinic may charge a late cancellation fee of $25.

If a patient misses an appointment without informing the clinic, it is considered a no-show. After three no-shows, the patient may be asked to speak with the clinic administrator before booking another appointment.

Insurance information must be submitted before the visit. If insurance cannot be verified, the patient may be asked to pay out of pocket.

For billing questions, patients can contact the billing department Monday through Friday between 9 AM and 5 PM.

Referral requests usually take 3 to 5 business days to process.
